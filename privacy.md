# Privacy Policy for Lyhn

**Effective Date:** April 2, 2026

Lyhn is a personal headache tracking app. This Privacy Policy explains what information Lyhn processes, how it is used, and what choices you have.

## 1. What Lyhn processes

Lyhn may process the following categories of information:

- Headache journal data you enter, such as dates, times, intensity, triggers, treatments, pain areas, pain type, and notes.
- Health data you explicitly authorize through Apple HealthKit, such as heart rate, respiratory rate, sleep, wrist temperature, oxygen saturation, heart rate variability, and headache events saved to Health.
- Precise location, if you grant location permission, to retrieve weather conditions related to a headache entry.
- Weather data returned for the time and place of an entry.
- News content, RSS feed metadata, images, and article links from public headache and migraine sources.
- A Sign in with Apple user identifier stored locally on your device so the app can check sign-in state.

## 2. How Lyhn uses this information

Lyhn uses this information to:

- Let you create, view, edit, delete, and export headache records.
- Correlate headache entries with optional HealthKit data and optional weather data.
- Sync your app data across your devices using Apple services where enabled.
- Show headache and migraine news from public RSS feeds.
- Support notifications, badge counts, session management, and security-related app behavior.

## 3. Where data is stored

- Headache records, notes, weather records, and related app data are stored locally on your device and may be synced through your private Apple iCloud / CloudKit storage.
- HealthKit data remains subject to Apple’s Health and HealthKit protections. Lyhn only reads or writes Health data you authorize.
- Lyhn does not operate its own dedicated backend for storing your headache journal data.

## 4. Third-party and Apple services

Lyhn relies on the following external services:

- Apple services, including iCloud / CloudKit, HealthKit, Sign in with Apple, Keychain, and UserNotifications.
- Visual Crossing, which receives precise location and time-range request data when Lyhn fetches weather for an entry.
- Public RSS and publisher-hosted news sources, which provide article metadata, images, and links shown in the app.

When you open an external article or interact with third-party content, that third party’s privacy practices apply.

## 5. What Lyhn does not do

Lyhn does not use third-party advertising SDKs.  
Lyhn does not sell your personal data.  
Lyhn does not use your data for third-party advertising or cross-app tracking.  
Lyhn does not collect crash analytics or behavioral analytics through third-party analytics SDKs in the current app version.

## 6. User controls

You can:

- Decline or revoke HealthKit permission in Apple Health or Settings.
- Decline or revoke Location permission in Settings.
- Decline notifications in Settings.
- Delete headache entries inside the app where deletion is supported.
- Export your data using the app’s export features.
- Sign out of the app.
- Remove app data from your device and manage iCloud data through your Apple account settings.

## 7. Retention and deletion

Lyhn keeps your data until you delete it, remove the app data from your device or iCloud account, or revoke access to the relevant Apple services. Exported files that you save or share outside the app are controlled by you and the destination you choose.

## 8. Security

Lyhn uses Apple platform security features, including app sandboxing, iCloud / CloudKit protections, and Keychain storage for local credentials or tokens where applicable. No method of storage or transmission is guaranteed to be completely secure.

## 9. Children

Lyhn is not intended for children under 13.

## 10. Medical disclaimer

Lyhn is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical decisions.
