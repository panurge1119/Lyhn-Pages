## Common topics

### The app does not open or crashes
- Make sure your iPhone is running a supported version of iOS.
- Fully close the app and reopen it.
- Restart your iPhone and try again.

### Animations seem slow
- Close other apps using heavy system resources.
- Restart the app.
- Make sure your device is updated to the latest available iOS version.
