# Lyhn

Lyhn is a personal headache tracking app that helps you record headache episodes, review patterns over time, and export a clear history to discuss with your doctor.

## Quick Links

- [Support](./support-2)
- [Privacy Policy](./privacy-2)

## What Lyhn Helps You Do

- Log headache episodes with timing, intensity, triggers, treatments, pain areas, and notes
- Review headache history and identify patterns over time
- Export records when you want to share them with a healthcare professional
- Read headache and migraine news from public sources
